Vaultic
Powered by Sage

Your Personal Financial Command Center

Vaultic is a self-hosted personal finance dashboard that aggregates all your accounts in one place — bank accounts, investments, retirement, crypto, and mortgage — with a built-in AI financial advisor named Sage who has real-time access to your complete financial picture.

🏦 Plaid-connected accounts — Chase, Vanguard, Robinhood, and more
🤖 Sage AI advisor — ask anything about your finances
📈 Net worth history — daily snapshots, multi-year charts
📄 PDF import — parse investment statements automatically
🔐 JWT + bcrypt + TOTP 2FA — security-first from day one
🎙️ Voice interface — Hey Sage wake word + push-to-talk
Personal use · Self-hosted · Open source
